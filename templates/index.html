Guide: What is the AI Digital Twin & Agent and How to Use It?

What is the AI Digital Twin? The digital twin is a real-time behavioral representation of your placement readiness. It models five distinct core vectors: DSA Skill Level, Aptitude Speed, HR Confidence, Communication Ability, and Resume Quality. The values automatically update after your interactions with other tabs on the dashboard.

What is the Autonomous Agent? The agent scans hiring boards and analyzes interview data. It uses the digital twin profile to calculate your matching probabilities against different pipeline steps (e.g. Resume screen, online tests, and managerial rounds) for target companies.

How to Use it:

  • Attempt simulated questions in the AI Coding IDE or practice responses in the AI Interview Simulator to improve your twin's scores.
  • Directly browse and upload your resume in the Resume & JD Analyzer to calculate matching and ATS scores.
  • The probabilities updates instantly as the agent compiles new data.

      PrepOS AI - User Guide & Reference Manual

      🚀 Core Architecture & Active Modules Reference

      PrepOS AI organizes placement preparation into an automated, AI-driven operating system designed to guide students from any engineering branch (CSE, EEE, ECE, Mech, Civil) to job offers. Below is the manual detailing how each of our active modules functions:

      1. Placement Guide & PYQ Hub

      How to use: Manually type your Target Company (e.g., 'TCS') and exact Branch into the free-text fields. Select your interview category and click 'Analyze'. The AI deeply analyzes your custom input to fetch the correct syllabus, hiring structures, and PYQs.

      2. AI Digital Twin & Readiness Agent

      How to use: Navigate to the Digital Twin tab. Type in your current skills and target role. The AI evaluates your custom profile to simulate a readiness grid and predict your clearing probability.

      3. AI Interview Simulator

      How to use: Type your target Company, Role, and preferred Difficulty level manually into the text inputs. The AI crafts a highly specific mock interview tailored exactly to your unstructured input.

      4. Hiring Pattern Analyzer

      How to use: Enter your Target Company and Branch in the free-text fields. The AI parses your query to generate visual charts breaking down hiring structures and topic weightages.

      5. ATS Resume & JD Matcher

      How to use: Paste your full Resume text and the Job Description into the text boxes. The AI deeply analyzes the exact text provided to calculate match percentages and find missing keywords.

      6. Personalized Roadmap Generator

      How to use: Manually type your Branch, Target Subject, Timeframe, and Target Company. The AI analyzes these open-ended fields to build a highly customized, step-by-step preparation calendar.

      7. AI Coding IDE & Debugger

      How to use: Type your preferred programming language manually into the language field and paste your code. The AI engine traces your logic to debug boundary errors.

      8. AI Doubt Solver & Revision Flashcards

      How to use: Type any complex CS concept or doubt into the chat. The Copilot deeply analyzes your natural language query to generate spaced-repetition flashcards and explanations.

      9. Active Job Opportunities Feed

      How to use: Manually enter your Branch, Experience level, and Qualifications. The AI filters the dynamically updated job feed based on your specific custom text criteria.

      10. Active Abroad Opportunities Feed

      How to use: Type your Target Country, Branch, and Experience into the text fields. The AI cross-references international job boards to find visa-sponsored positions matching your query.

      11. Competitive Exams Feed

      How to use: Type your Branch, Experience, and Qualifications manually. The AI deeply searches for national/international exam bulletins that match your specific text.

      12. AI Book Store & Placement Library

      How to use: Manually type your Subject and preferred Mode (e.g., PDF, Hardcopy). The AI searches the library to fetch and download reference engineering textbooks matching your query.

      13. AI Portfolio Generator

      How to use: Enter your links and resume text. The AI dynamically generates a beautiful, hosted developer portfolio website based entirely on your custom inputs.

      14. Company Sheet PDF Generator

      How to use: Type the Company Name and your exact requirements. The AI compiles authentic placement preparation sheets in PDF format using real-time search context.

      15. AI Relocation Assistant

      How to use: Manually type your Target City. The AI deeply analyzes the city to provide transit routes, rental indexes, and corporate office parks.

      16. 1-Page Company Cheat Sheet

      How to use: Type the Company Name. The AI instantly generates a 2-minute revision sheet with key metrics, tech stacks, and recent news.

      17. PrepOS Predictor

      How to use: Manually enter your Branch, CGPA, and Skillset. The AI calculates your campus placement probability and audits your specific skill gaps.

      18. MMMUT Placement Statistics

      How to use: Access the official placement brochure and recruitment dashboard for 2026-27 to track college-specific placement metrics.

      19. Indian Tax & Salary Advisor

      How to use: Type your Base Salary, PF preference, and State. The AI deeply calculates structured in-hand salaries and personal tax plans based on your custom inputs.

      20. PrepOS AI User Guide

      How to use: You're reading it! Browse this manual to understand how the AI deeply analyzes your manual free-text inputs across all 30 modules.

      21. Vocal Stress Heatmap

      How to use: Conduct a mock interview. The AI analyzes your speech speed and filler words in real-time to map where your communication breaks down.

      22. Script Doctor (60-Sec Elevator Pitch)

      How to use: Manually type your Target Role, Superpower (e.g., 'Backend Scaling'), and desired Vibe. The AI generates a perfectly timed, 130-word conversational pitch based on your exact text.

      23. Negotiation Arena

      How to use: Type your Company Name, Base Salary, and Leverage (e.g., 'Competing Offer'). The AI drafts a highly personalized counter-offer email based on your custom scenario.

      24. Cold Mail Generator

      How to use: Manually type the Recipient's Role, your Connection to them, and your Ask. The AI parses your unstructured text to write a punchy, 75-word outreach email.

      25. Email Etiquette Doctor

      How to use: Paste your rough draft email and manually type your desired Tone (e.g., 'Polite & Assertive'). The AI instantly polishes the exact text into your requested tone.

      26. Skill Gap Radar

      How to use: Type your Target Role and paste your current tech stack. The AI deeply analyzes your custom text against industry requirements to identify missing skills.

      27. Ghost Detector

      How to use: Type the Company Name and the Size of the company. The AI compares your post-interview wait time against company medians to determine if you are stalled.

      28. LinkedIn Studio

      How to use: Manually type your Target Domain and preferred Tone. The AI generates recruiter-optimized, keyword-rich headlines and an engaging 'About' section for your specific input.

      29. Endorsement Scripting

      How to use: Type your Relationship to the recommender and the Platform. The AI drafts low-friction request emails for Letters of Recommendation using your exact parameters.

      30. Referral Request Gen

      How to use: Manually type the Job ID and your Connection to the referrer. The AI drafts a brief, 3-sentence referral request targeting your specific context.
