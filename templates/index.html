Guide: What is the AI Digital Twin & Agent and How to Use It?

What is the AI Digital Twin? The digital twin is a real-time behavioral representation of your placement readiness. It models five distinct core vectors: DSA Skill Level, Aptitude Speed, HR Confidence, Communication Ability, and Resume Quality. The values automatically update after your interactions with other tabs on the dashboard.

What is the Autonomous Agent? The agent scans hiring boards and analyzes interview data. It uses the digital twin profile to calculate your matching probabilities against different pipeline steps (e.g. Resume screen, online tests, and managerial rounds) for target companies.

How to Use it:

  • Attempt simulated questions in the AI Coding IDE or practice responses in the AI Interview Simulator to improve your twin's scores.
  • Directly browse and upload your resume in the Resume & JD Analyzer to calculate matching and ATS scores.
  • The probabilities updates instantly as the agent compiles new data.

      PrepOS AI - Operating System Reference Manual

      🚀 Core Architecture & 60 Flagship Features Reference

      PrepOS AI organizes placement preparation into an automated, AI-driven operating system designed to guide students from any branch (CSE, EEE, ECE, Mech, Civil, Core) to job offers. Below is the guide explaining how all our integrated modules work:

      1. AI Interview Simulator

      Simulates voice and text interviews. Scoring adjusts dynamically based on technical depth, vocabulary, communication efficiency, thinking speed, and grammar.

      2. Hiring Pattern Analyzer

      Parses placement requirements, selection rates, topic weightages (e.g., Arrays 30%, DP 20%), average round count, and difficulty trends.

      3. AI Resume Analyzer

      Inspects resume texts for keyword deficiencies, matches achievements, and provides an ATS score out of 100 with optimization logs.

      4. Job Description Matcher

      Calculates matching percentage between job descriptions and resumes, reporting missing skills and key technologies.

      5. Roadmap Generator

      Constructs study roadmaps complete with daily schedules, weekly goals, monthly milestones, and revision calendars adjusted to your branch.

      6. Embedded Coding IDE

      Provides a code playground with syntax inputs supporting C++, Java, Python, and JavaScript, tracking hidden test cases.

      7. AI Code Debugger

      Inspects user-provided code, highlighting logic bugs, boundary edge cases, and proposing cleaner algorithmic solutions.

      8. Company Sheet Generator

      Automatically schedules preparation sheets outlining target DSA questions, SQL queries, and core topics (OS, CN, DBMS).

      9. Interview Experience Aggregator

      Gathers student reviews and round-wise interview logs, summarizing tips and actual placement questions.

      10. AI Doubt Solver

      Resolves concepts, traces algorithmic complexities, and presents step-by-step animations or logic breakdowns.

      11. Placement Analytics Dashboard

      Monitors problems solved, accuracy graphs, daily streaks, weak areas, and score progress.

      12. Spaced-Repetition Flashcards

      Dynamically creates revision cards covering OS, DBMS, computer networks, and aptitude logic.

      13. Mock Online Assessment (OA)

      Simulates real-world coding exams with countdown timers, test assertions, and post-completion reviews.

      14. Company Comparison Matrix

      Performs side-by-side comparison between companies (e.g. Google vs Amazon) covering packages, selection rates, and skill maps.

      15. AI Roadmap Visualizer

      Generates node diagrams mapping preparation milestones (Arrays -> Trees -> System Design) unlocking progressively.

      16. Live Placement News

      Monitors off-campus opportunities, open referral postings, internship openings, and competitive hackathons.

      17. AI HR Coach

      Analyzes and evaluates responses to standard behavioral prompts like "Tell me about yourself", scoring professionalism and length.

      18. Behavioral Questions Trainer

      Structures interview answers using the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) for leadership scenarios.

      19. Company Knowledge Hub

      Displays company profiles, tech stacks, core engineering culture, and detailed salary structures.

      20. Referral Finder

      Constructs and structures professional referral request messages for LinkedIn or email outreach.

      21. AI Placement Digital Twin

      Continuously updated profile that predicts candidate readiness across DSA level, aptitude speed, communication, and interview metrics.

      22. AI Hiring Probability Engine

      Estimates likelihood of clearing each recruitment stage (Resume, OA, Tech, Managerial) based on history.

      23. AI Interview Memory

      Retains historical practice details, avoiding repeating questions and focusing on weak topics.

      24. Multi-Agent AI Architecture

      Orchestrates requests across dedicated agents (Resume Agent, Mock Interviewer, Recruiter Simulator).

      25. Autonomous Web Intelligence

      Continuously pulls public logs to discover newly trending placement questions and pattern changes.

      26. Company Knowledge Graph

      27. AI Strategy Generator

      28. Adaptive Difficulty Engine

      29. AI Error Pattern Detection
