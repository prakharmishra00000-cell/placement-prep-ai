Guide: What is the AI Digital Twin & Agent and How to Use It?

What is the AI Digital Twin? The digital twin is a real-time behavioral representation of your placement readiness. It models five distinct core vectors: DSA Skill Level, Aptitude Speed, HR Confidence, Communication Ability, and Resume Quality. The values automatically update after your interactions with other tabs on the dashboard.

What is the Autonomous Agent? The agent scans hiring boards and analyzes interview data. It uses the digital twin profile to calculate your matching probabilities against different pipeline steps (e.g. Resume screen, online tests, and managerial rounds) for target companies.

How to Use it:

  • Attempt simulated questions in the AI Coding IDE or practice responses in the AI Interview Simulator to improve your twin's scores.
  • Directly browse and upload your resume in the Resume & JD Analyzer to calculate matching and ATS scores.
  • The probabilities updates instantly as the agent compiles new data.

      PrepOS AI - User Guide & Reference Manual

      🚀 Core Architecture & Active Modules Reference

      PrepOS AI organizes placement preparation into an automated, AI-driven operating system designed to guide students from any engineering branch (CSE, EEE, ECE, Mech, Civil) to job offers. Below is the manual detailing how each of our active modules functions:

      1. Placement Guide & PYQ Hub

      Retrieve detailed placement syllabus, hiring structures, salary packages, and previous year questions (PYQs) for any target company instantly using Google Gemini.

      2. AI Digital Twin & Readiness Agent

      Simulate candidate readiness score grids. Evaluates DSA coding strength, communication depth, core subjects knowledge, and predicts company clearing probability.

      3. AI Interview Simulator

      Perform mock technical and behavioral interviews. Offers live chat-based feedback, answers evaluations, communication clarity ratings, and diagnostic scorecards.

      4. Hiring Pattern Analyzer

      View visual charts breaking down hiring structures, topic weightages (e.g. Arrays vs DP), and interview rounds count history for target companies.

      5. ATS Resume & JD Matcher

      Check resume ATS compatibility score out of 100. Paste job descriptions to calculate structural skills match percentages and find missing keywords.

      6. Personalized Roadmap Generator

      Creates step-by-step preparation calendars complete with daily study blocks, weekly targets, and monthly milestones adapted to your engineering branch.

      7. AI Coding IDE & Debugger

      Write programming solutions in the code playground. Features compiler runs against test cases and AI-powered logic tracing to debug boundary errors.

      8. AI Doubt Solver & Revision Flashcards

      Resolve computer science doubts instantly. Generates spaced-repetition flashcards covering OS, Computer Networks, and DBMS for quick revision.

      9. Active Job Opportunities Feed

      A 24/7 automated dashboard showing fresh local job postings tagged with branch, required experience, qualification filters, and direct application links.

      10. Active Abroad Opportunities Feed

      Tracks international job postings, visa sponsorship opportunities, and overseas career platforms for all branches and countries.

      11. Active Competitive Exams Feed

      Monitors national and global competitive exam bulletins (such as GATE, UPSC, ISRO, IES/ESE, and GRE/GMAT) with dynamic date badges.
